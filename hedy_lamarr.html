Scientist Spotlight
1914 - 2000

Hedy Lamarr

Pioneered wifi technology, famed actress

  • Often called the “Mother of WiFi”
  • German actress, fled to the United States after marrying a Nazi arms dealer
  • Got an offer in Hollywood, and immediately negotiated a 400% salary increase
  • Marketed as “the world's most beautiful woman”
  • Self-taught engineer
  • Invented improvements to the stoplight
  • Invented carbonation tablets
  • Worked on torpedo guidance systems, using her knowledge from her marriage to the arms dealer, to fight the Axis powers
  • Once arrested for shoplifting laxatives and eyedrops
  • Pioneered the technology used in WiFi, GPS, and Bluetooth, frequency hopping
