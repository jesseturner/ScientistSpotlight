Scientist Spotlight
1819 - 1888

Eunice Foote

Discovered the greenhouse gas effect

  • First demonstrated the theory of carbon dioxide having a global warming effect
  • Invented the thermometer-controlled stove, non-squeaking shoes, and a paper-making machine
  • Distant relative of Isaac Newton
  • Active campaigner for women's rights
  • Described as a skilled portrait and landscape painter
